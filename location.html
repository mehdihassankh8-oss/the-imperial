Transportation

Getting Here

Multiple convenient ways to reach Hotel Imperial

From the Airport

A short drive from Jinnah International Airport. We offer airport pickup service — contact us in advance to arrange your transfer. Typical travel time is 15–20 minutes depending on traffic.

From City Center

Both our DHA and PECHS locations are easily accessible from major roads and highways. Use ride-hailing apps like Careem or InDriver, or take a taxi directly to either location.

Nearby Landmarks

Our DHA branch is near Zamzama and Khadda Market. The PECHS branch is close to Tariq Road and Bahadurabad. Both locations are well-connected to Karachi's main attractions.
